About WeRockIT

Happening in May 2019!

A premier event. WeRockIT Conf is an annual two-day conference that features all disciplines of technology and shows how they all can interconnect. We'll highlight a wide range of speakers to educate and inspire our guests. Our mission is to showcase and grow diverse voices in technology.

This conference is being organized by Women Who Code Huntsville. WWCode Huntsville is a group of developers,engineers, and business professionals in the Huntsville area. WeRockIT Conf will include a mix of panel discussions, keynotes, speakers, and hands-on workshops.
